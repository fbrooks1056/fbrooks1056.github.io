Hobbies

Gaming

Gaming has been one of my main hobbies for a long time. I enjoy games such as World of Warcraft, Diablo, Path of Exile, and Halo. I like both the social side of games and the way they combine programming, art, sound, and design into one experience.

Music and Guitar

Music is another major interest of mine. I play guitar and enjoy learning about amps, pedals, recording equipment, and digital audio tools. Some of my personal project ideas also connect music and technology, such as building tools for tracking concerts or experimenting with virtual guitar signal chains.

Gaming Through the Generations

I have loved video games since the Nintendo 64 era and have owned consoles from nearly every major generation through the PlayStation 5. Gaming has been a consistent hobby throughout my life, and I enjoy seeing how graphics, controls, online features, and game design have changed over time. I still enjoy revisiting older games while also keeping up with newer releases.

Supper Clubs, Cards, and Comedy

Outside of gaming, I enjoy visiting Wisconsin supper clubs, playing the card game Jokers, and watching comedy with my girlfriend, Alicia. These are some of my favorite ways to relax, spend time together, and take a break from work and school.
